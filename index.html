About catchR

catchR is an AI-powered computer vision program designed to detect guns in videos. With the increasing numbers of school shootings, catchR aims to protect schools by providing an advanced gun detection system.

Features

  • Accurate Gun Detection

    Our advanced AI algorithms accurately detect guns in videos, providing real-time alerts to prevent potential threats.

  • Enhanced School Security

    By integrating catchR into school surveillance systems, we ensure a safer environment for students and faculty.

  • Easy Integration

    catchR can be seamlessly integrated into existing video monitoring systems, making it accessible for schools of all sizes.

catchR is Free!

Our AI works off of video input. Linked is our github repository, where the code is stored alongside a sample video to showcase the use of our AI, with more instruction in a README file: https://github.com/sawgur/catchR/

Why choose catchR?

catchR is the solution to engaging safer communities. As school shootings in America rise, communities and families are distraught. catchR hopes to help.

  • Proactive Threat Prevention

    With catchR's accurate gun detection capabilities, potential threats can be identified in real-time, allowing for quick response and prevention of school shootings.

  • Enhanced Safety Measures

    By integrating catchR into school surveillance systems, administrators can improve overall safety measures and provide a secure environment for students and faculty.

  • Efficient Resource Utilization

    catchR's easy integration with existing video monitoring systems ensures efficient utilization of resources, making it accessible for schools of all sizes without significant infrastructure changes.
